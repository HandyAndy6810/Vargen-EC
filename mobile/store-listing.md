## Description (4000 chars max)
Vargen is the job management app built for Australian tradespeople who'd rather be on the tools than buried in admin.

Quote, invoice, and schedule jobs from your phone — in the time it takes to finish a coffee.

**QUOTES IN SECONDS**
Stop re-typing the same lines. Vargen's AI learns your rates, materials, and style, then drafts a professional quote from a quick voice note or a few taps. Send it to your customer with a single button. They accept or decline from their phone — no printing, no PDFs, no back-and-forth.

**INVOICES THAT GET PAID**
Turn an accepted quote into an invoice instantly. Send it with a Stripe payment link so customers can pay by card on the spot. Automatic reminders chase overdue invoices so you don't have to. Sync everything to Xero so your books stay tidy without a second thought.

**JOB SCHEDULING**
See your full week at a glance. Tap a job to start a timer, log your hours, and mark it complete with notes — all from the same screen. The calendar shows where you need to be and when, with an "Up next" card so you're never hunting for the address.

**BUILT FOR TRADIES**
- Electricians, plumbers, builders, and more
- GST-inclusive quoting with your labour rate and markup
- Customer database with job history
- Quote follow-up reminders via SMS or email
- Xero sync for contacts and invoices
- Card payments via Stripe

**PRIVACY FIRST**
Your data stays yours. No ads, no selling your customer list.

Download Vargen and take back your evenings.
